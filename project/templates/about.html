1. We send you the game

We email you 10 questions each Wednesday between 11am and noon EST. You have until that Friday at 11:59pm to answer the questions.

2. Fill out your answers

The goal is to guess the MOST COMMON answer to a question. Do you go with your gut or defy it? You can Google it, but no discussing answers with other players!

3. Find yourself on the leaderboard

We tabulate the results and publish the leaderboard on Monday. You get one point for everyone that answers the same as you on each question.

4. Tell all your friends

You probably did really well so you should rub it in. Or maybe you can't believe people actually like grape soda. The game makes a great icebreaker!
