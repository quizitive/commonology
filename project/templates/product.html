What do we have in common? Or, what do we think we have in common?

Commonology is a weekly game where players compete to answer life's most important questions.
  • When people think of fish… what kind do they think of?
  • Is a hamburger a sandwich?
  • What has the best cast: Friends, Seinfeld, Parks and Recreation, or The Office?
We email you 10 questions each Wednesday between 11am and noon EST. You have until that Friday at 11:59pm to answer the questions.
Answer questions each week and find out how common your answers are. Anyone can play, you only need an opinion. You might THINK you know what the most popular answer will be… but the answers from strangers, your closest friends - even your family will surprise you!
Many families and groups of friends play every week. Makes for great conversation. A social experience.
BEWARE: You may end up talking to your friends and family about things other than news events or politics.
