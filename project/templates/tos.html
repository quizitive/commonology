Free Response or Open Ended Responses

A game host is someone who is responsible for running a game consisting of question selection and scoring.

Our game often has questions that invite an open ended response, any text a player wishes to enter. We will use those responses to score the game. Our methodology consists of things like categorizing responses so that more than one response my be treated as the same. For example if one player enters "pen" and another player enters "fountain pen" we may consider those equal answers. Categorization is a machine assisted human effort. Our game host or scorer will use their discretion at times to do the best and fairest job according to their ability.

If a players enters inappropriate or offensive responses the host may at their discretion disqualify that question for the play and choose to ignore their responses or even disqualify the player from the game entirely.
